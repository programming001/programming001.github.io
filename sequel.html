Sequel Triology


The Sequel Triology (2015-2019) follows Rey, a young woman discovering her abilities in the force, and her journey to assist the

Rebel Resistance against the First Order. The known antagonist is Kylo Ren, a recently darkside turned student of Master Luke Skywalker.

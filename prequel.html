Prequel Triology


The Prequel Triology (1999-2005) follows Anakin Skywalker in his journey to defend the Galactic Republic as a Jedi Knight.

All three films focus on his eventual turn to the darkside of the force, and fall of the republic. Key events include the Clone Wars,

Purge of the Jedi Order, and Rise of the Galactic Empire

Original Triology


The Original Triology (1977-1983) follows Luke Skywalker in his journey to take down the evil Galactic Empire and restore freedom to the Galaxy. The primary

antagonist for the first two films is Darth Vader, however the introduction of the Emperor shows Vader's status as antihero. The film events occur approximately

20 years after the fall of the Old Republic in the Prequel Triology

      A New Hope (1977)
      The Expire Strikes Back (1981)
      Return of the Jedi (1983)

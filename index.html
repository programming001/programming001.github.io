History


Star Wars is a film series created by George Lucas, it is centered on a select group of characters in their struggle to control a galaxy far, far away.

The three film triologies form a nine film saga following the Skywalker family and the effect they have on the Galaxy. Lucas was inspired by the old serials of the past,

and used his first film as a breakout for the science fiction genre and it became the highest grossing film of all time until the mid 1980s.
